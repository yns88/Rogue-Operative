I'm not making this game for profit, but if you'd like to encourage me to make progress on this game, then giving me a little money is a great way to do it. As of now, donating will not get you anything special, but if I choose to change that policy (such as giving X reward for all donations $Y or greater) then all past donations that fit the policy will retroactively receive that reward too, regardless of whether the donator's name was publicly listed.

Disclaimer: I intend to keep this game free and open source. Access to the game's files and source are available to everyone regardless of donator status.
